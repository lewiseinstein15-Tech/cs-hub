🌱 Year 1 Foundation

The first stage of the Computer Science ecosystem. These units build the mathematical, programming, and computing foundations required for advanced topics such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Software Engineering.

Year 1 · Semester 1

01 · COMPUTER SCIENCE

Introduction to Computer Science

Introduction to computing concepts, history of computers, hardware, software, algorithms and the foundations of computer science.

02 · PROGRAMMING

Programming

Problem solving, algorithms, programming concepts, data types, control structures, functions and software development basics.

03 · DISCRETE STRUCTURES I

Discrete Structures I

Logic, sets, relations, functions, Boolean algebra and mathematical foundations of computing.

04 · MATHEMATICS

Calculus I

Limits, differentiation, integration and mathematical tools used in computing.

05 · STATISTICS

Statistics and Probability I

Probability concepts, data analysis and statistical methods used in computer science.

06 · ELECTRONICS

Electronics

Basic electronic principles, components and digital systems.

07 · ELECTRICAL PRINCIPLES

Electrical Principles

Fundamentals of electricity, circuits and electrical systems.

08 · COMMUNICATION SKILLS

Communication Skills

Academic writing, presentations and professional communication for computing students.
